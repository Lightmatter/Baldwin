About Michael

Michael Baldwin is an accomplished leader in the communications industry with more than 25 years of experience building global brands, leading global teams, solving complex strategic problems, and developing world-class creative campaigns.

He is the founder and President of MICHAEL BALDWIN INC., which offers personal and corporate branding, executive and team coaching, and one-of-a-kind PowerPoint training that turns corporate presenters into truly effective communicators. Simply put, Michael helps individuals, teams, companies, and brands Be better®.

Michael has developed an entirely original way of enabling people to put the "power" back in PowerPoint while at the same time putting the focus of a presentation back where it belongs - on the presenter. By combining his actor's training with deep expertise in advertising and branding, Michael is able to create a powerful, transformative experience that shows his trainees how to move audiences by being themselves.

Michael spent eight years with Ogilvy & Mather, where he was the Worldwide Account Director for the $500 million IBM account. He also managed the SAP account, directing the integrated communications campaign and building a global team in over 40 countries.

He is a highly decorated professional, having won the David Ogilvy Award for his leadership on the SAP global brand campaign, the Cannes Gold Lion, a Clio, D&AD Silver, and Ad Age's "Best of the Year" TV Campaign award.

Michael has held posts at other top agencies: Ammirati & Puris, BBDO and FCB. There he headed up campaigns for such clients as: Apple, Compaq, A&W Beverages and NeXT.

Michael was previously the managing director of IDology, a London-based training consultancy. While there, he led executive coaching sessions on leadership, building global teams, presentation skills, and maximizing individual performance and impact. He also transformed every aspect of its branding, identity, website, and coaching materials so as to better convey the IDology message.

Michael studied professional acting at the William Esper Studio and the Deena Levy Theater Studio in New York. He is a graduate of Beloit College in Wisconsin and currently resides in New York City.
